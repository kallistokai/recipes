# Lángos

#### Yields 12 medium sized lángos

## Dough
- 1 kg wheat flour type 405
- 400 ml warm milk
- 200 ml warm water
- 1 cube (42 g) of fresh yeast or 2 bags of dry yeast
- 2 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp salt

## Toppings
- 400 g sour cream
- 125 g diced bacon
- 150 g gouda cheese
- 1 feta cheese
- 2 spring onions

## Garlic water
- 1 bulb of garlic
- 1 tsp salt
- water
- oil
